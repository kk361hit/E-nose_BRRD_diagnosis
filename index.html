E-nose BRRD diagnose tool

This rapid diagnostic system includes an portable E-nose with 32 channels and a machine learning-based tool for rapid diagnosis of brown rot root disease in trees.

Bronw rot root disease (BRRD)

Recently Brown Root Rot disease (BRRD) caused by fungal pathogen Phellinus noxius (also denoted as P. noxius) was spotted with increased frequency. This disease results in rapid health and structural deterioration of trees and may lead to tree failure, thus posting a serious threat to public safety.

The spread of the fungal infection is mainly via root encrustations over short distances and mutated basidiospores are majorly responsible for long-distance spread by establishing clones at the new infection sites. The direct infection is caused through fresh wounds by basidiospores and indirectly via colonizing plant debris and extending to infect the roots of a surrounding plant.

The deterioration may lead to the tree collapsing and may affect human activities by posing danger around private and public roadsides, parks, and buildings.
